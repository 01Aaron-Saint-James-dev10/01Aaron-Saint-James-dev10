## What I am building

**[Simplifii-OS](https://simplifii-os-main.vercel.app)** — a neuroinclusive academic operating system for students whose brains work differently.

Not a study app. Infrastructure for surviving education when the system was never designed for you.

It decodes assessment briefs, surfaces hidden curriculum, guides students through their actual documents phase by phase, and produces an Authenticity Report that proves the work was theirs — so AI assistance is transparent and defensible, not hidden.

Built for: ADHD, dyslexia, autism, CFS, C-PTSD, processing differences, and anyone who has ever hidden their difficulty in a classroom.

Architecture: React 18 · Supabase · Vercel · Anthropic Claude · UDL 3.0

## What I believe about AI in education

AI is being marketed to students as a shortcut. I do not think students need a shortcut. I think they need a tool that respects how they actually think.

The product enforces this architecturally:
- Every AI suggestion is logged and traceable
- The student is always the author — AI assists, never replaces
- The Authenticity Report documents the genuine thinking process
- Institutions see aggregate patterns, never individual work
